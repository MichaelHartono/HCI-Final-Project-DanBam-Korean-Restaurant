- About Us -

DanBam is a Korean restaurant that has been established since the 1979. We have been serving our dishes to several generation of families and has gained widespread recognition.

- Our History -

DanBam was founded in 1979 by Jang Joon-Soo. Our first restaurant was in Seoul and was only a simple family restaurant at its founding.

Due to our excellence, DanBam quickly became the go-to spot for the best Korean food. Due to our overwhelming success, we now have opened several branches in Seoul.
