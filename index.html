Naengmyeon

Naengmyeon is a Korean noodle dish of long and thin handmade noodles made from the flour and starch of various ingredients, including buckwheat, potatoes, sweet potatoes, arrowroot starch, and kudzu.

Baekseju

Baekseju is a herb-flavored Korean rice wine made with fermented glutinous rice. Ginseng is the dominant flavor and includes a blend of herbs and spices such as ginger, omija, goji berries, or cinnamon.

Gwapyeon

Gwapyeon is a traditional Korean dessert made with fruit. It is made by boiling fruit juice with honey, sugar, and starch.
